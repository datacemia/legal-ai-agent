GENERAL COMMERCIAL COOPERATION AGREEMENT
This General Commercial Cooperation Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of July 6, 2026, between [PARTY_1], a [LOCATION] limited liability company ("[PARTY_1]"), and [PARTY_2] ("[PARTY_2]"), for the purpose of establishing a general framework of commercial cooperation relating to product design, co-branding, and joint marketing initiatives.
"Cooperation Activities" means the joint design, co-branding, and marketing initiatives undertaken by the Parties pursuant to a mutually agreed project plan.
"Joint IP" means any intellectual property jointly developed by the Parties in the course of the Cooperation Activities.
1. SCOPE OF COOPERATION
1.1 The Parties shall collaborate on Cooperation Activities as mutually agreed from time to time in a written project plan, which shall specify the scope, timeline, budget, and responsibilities of each Party.
2. COSTS AND EXPENSES
2.1 Unless otherwise specified in a project plan, each Party shall bear its own costs and expenses incurred in connection with the Cooperation Activities. Any shared costs shall be split equally unless otherwise agreed in writing.
3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
3.1 Each Party retains ownership of its pre-existing intellectual property. [ORGANIZATION] shall be jointly owned by the Parties in equal, undivided shares, with each Party entitled to exploit the [ORGANIZATION] without accounting to the other, subject to a right of first negotiation before licensing [ORGANIZATION] to a direct competitor of the other Party.
4. BRANDING AND PUBLICITY
4.1 Neither Party shall use the [ORGANIZATION]'s trademarks, trade names, or branding in any publicity, marketing material, or press release without the [ORGANIZATION]'s prior written approval.
5. CONFIDENTIALITY
5.1 Each Party shall keep confidential any non-public information disclosed by the other Party in connection with this Agreement and shall use such information solely for purposes of the Cooperation Activities.
